Innovating UK entertainment supplyWhat we do

Established in 1978, Lasgo Worldwide Media has become the UK's longest established international wholesaler and distributor of home entertainment product.

From our humble beginnings in London's Oxford Street, our success has been achieved through the company's ability to diversify and identify new opportunities. As such we were well placed to take full advantage of this growth. We have also invested in the expansion of our Visual Entertainment and Merchandise divisions to meet a growing demand for fan-based physical products.
